opy That


Check It Out

Copy That allows you to click on any text and copy it to your clipboard.

Ex:

Click on The text Below to copy it

Copy this Text.

Now paste that in a new tab.

Usage

Inside your>

<head>

Tag in yourHTML, put this code:

<script src="https://talonbragg.github.io/CopyThat/dist/main.js"><script/>

Now all you need to do is give your tag that you want to copy with a click an id, like this:

<p id="copy"<Copy This Text With a Click<p/>
